About

Cricket is a bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of eleven players. The game involves two sets of three stumps (wickets) placed at each end of a rectangular pitch. One team bats, aiming to score runs by hitting the ball and running between the wickets or hitting the ball to the boundary, while the other team bowls and fields, trying to dismiss the batsmen and restrict their scoring. A match consists of one or two innings, with the objective being to score more runs than the opposing team. Cricket has different formats, including Test matches, one-day internationals, and T20 internationals.
